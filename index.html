Conservatives In Tech (CIT) is a growing, digital community whose chief aim is to use the skills and relationships cultivated over years of experience to help shape the future of the digital political landscape resulting in a stronger America.

The tech industry has become hostile to many who openly hold Conservative or Libertarian viewpoints. But we believe that the open exchange of ideas with a sincere inquiry after truth is vital to a thriving culture and intellectually-rich community.
