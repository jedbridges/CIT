Conservatives In Tech

United By Design

Conservatives In Tech (CIT) is a growing, invite-only, digital community whose chief aim is to use the skills and relationships cultivated over years of experience to help shape the future of the digital political landscape resulting in a stronger America.

The tech industry has become hostile to many who openly hold Conservative or Libertarian viewpoints. We believe that the open exchange of ideas with a sincere inquiry after truth is vital to a thriving culture and intellectually-rich community.

Conservatives In Tech is invite only.  If you’d like to be a part of this community, please click the button below and fill out the Google form to Request an Invite.
