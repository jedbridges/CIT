Conservatives In Tech

United By Design

Conservatives In Tech (CIT) is a growing, invitee-only, digital community for the mutual benefit of it’s members. We want to be a group that uses our skills and connections to help each other and make the world better.

The tech industry has become an increasingly hostile place to those who openly hold conservative or libertarian viewpoints. We hold that the open exchange of ideas in a sincere spirit of inquiry after truth is vital to our culture and civilization. Network could protect us, etc… We are in a battle together… Camaraderie…
